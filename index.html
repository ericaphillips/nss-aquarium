Fish Tips

Tank Salinity

All fish love salt. Pour an entire shaker in every day to ensure proper salinity. Do not worry about tank size; one shaker should be enough for any tank.

Tank Temperature

All fish love the cold. Kobe an ice cube in every 80 minutes to be safe.
